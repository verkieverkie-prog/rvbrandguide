What this is

The RV Brand Reliability Guide is our plain-English opinion, based on the public record: federal recall and investigation records, court filings, manufacturer warranty documents, and documented owner reports. We do our best to get every call right, we cite our sources so you can check them, and where the evidence is thin the entry says so. Brands change hands and facts change, so the guide is updated as they do.

Every rig is its own case

A brand verdict describes the pattern, not the unit. Any individual RV can be better or worse than its badge, which is why the guide keeps telling you to inspect the specific unit and hire an independent inspector before you sign. This is not legal or financial advice, and the buying decision is your own.
