Why this guide exists

The brochure says one thing. The federal recall record says another. Dealers count on you falling in love with a floor plan before you ever ask what is behind the walls, and nobody at the dealership is going to volunteer which brands are under federal investigation. So we check. Every verdict in this guide traces to a source you can verify yourself: federal recall and investigation records, court filings, manufacturer warranty documents, and documented owner reports.

No brand pays us. No dealer pays us. If a brand earns a buy, it gets a buy, whether they like us or not. Where the evidence is thin, the entry says so plainly. And when we get one wrong, we correct it in the open.

The year matters more than the badge

Most brand advice fails because it gives one flat answer for a company that changed hands twice and changed factories once. The truth splits by model year, and that is exactly how this guide is written. The same badge can be a buy in 2018 and a walk-away in 2021. We tell you which years, and we show you why.
